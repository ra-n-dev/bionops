About

Will you like to know much about Bio-nops company Ltd?.

Bio-Nops Company Ltd. started on the 15th of July 2018. This company was formed to reduce the energy fluctuation and fire outbreak problems hiting Ghana due to imporper electric cable wiring and overload on electrical appliancies. Bio-Nops Company Ltd. thought of improving Ghana's renewable energy sector and also providing proper electrical wiring and connections. Many companies and households depend on the ECG for power supply and these has placed so much load on the Hydro Electric power system. We are a registered company in Ghana. We are located in Accra Kwabenya to be precise. The company size is 11 persons with three administrative workers and seven field workers with one Marketing director.We do electrical works such as house wiring, metre fixing, fan fixing etc. We do solar panel installation, solar maintanace, etc. We do Biogas Digester installation, solar maintanace, etc. Our priority has always been our clients. To offer them quality service on a cheap budget.

Our achievements?.

We have provided services for countless individuals and institutions. Just to mention few, we installed a 20cubic biogas digester for Teshie Technical Institute, We installed biogas digester for Gborgame Community in the volta region, we installed solar panel for UCC physics department, we did electrical works for some countless people in Accra and beyond. With us your problem is solved.

Our mission going forward?.

We intend to sell the idea of renewable energy out to the entire nation and beyond. It is also our goal to reduce the rate of fire outbreak and ensure proper electrical wiring in the city of Accra and beyond. Finally we intend to create the avenue to teach and train as many interested individuals on our technology.
